Security First also offers training and services in a number of areas.

RISK ASSESSMENT

Comprehensive assessment of your organisation’s security – covers physical and cyber security, levels of risk and how to mitigate it.

PENETRATION TESTING

A test of your organisation’s cyber-security defences – we conduct penetration tests (ethical hacking), to find your organisation’s digital weak spots and recommend solutions.

PHYSICAL SECURITY

Organisational and operational physical security training – includes how to secure your office, secure meetings, conduct counter-surveillance, deal with arrest and evacuation.



DIGITAL SECURITY

Organisational and operational cyber-security training – includes how to securely communicate, access the internet and encrypt and store data.

RESEARCH SKILLS

Advanced research skills for NGOs – based on the most advanced open source intelligence gathering methods.

RESEARCH MANAGEMENT

Advanced research management for NGOs – using advanced database tools to more effectively manage your research, to maximise the value of your information and increase your institutional knowledge.



FIRST AID

First aid training for your team – how to deal with everything from basic health to advanced trauma.

CLOSE PROTECTION

For NGOs working in areas of the highest risk – we can provide training and/or teams of experienced protection professionals to allow you to continue you with your work.

CRISIS MANAGEMENT

Testing your plans, team and management in a dynamic, realistic scenario tailored to your specific needs.
